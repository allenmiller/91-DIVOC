An interactive visualization of the exponential spread of COVID-19

A project to explore the global growth of COVID-19. Updated daily. Overview and motivations.
A few interesting bits I found interesting to explore:
  • These graphs are not just images. Hover over any data point to see the data behind it.
  • There are millions of combinations of graphs to view:
    • You can add multiple highlights and change view subsets of the data by editing options below each graph.
    • Switch between a linear and log scale by toggling the scale at the bottom of each graph.
    • Generate detailed data reports based off the visualization you create.
    • View all the graphs below or each graph individually: by countries, by US States, or population normalized by country, or by US state.
  • Latest Enhancement: (July 25) Improved data load failure detection. If you ever get a failure to load the visualization -- please let me know (contact information at bottom of page) [See full change log]
  • This visualization is updated daily from multiple data sources.
