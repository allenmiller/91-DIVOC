COVID-19 Data for Locations of People You Love

A project to display COVID-19 data only for the locations that matter most to you. Updated daily.
There is a lot of COVID-19 data out there. Increasingly, I have been focused on the locations where I have family and close friends.
  • Displays COVID-19 data for only the locations you select.
  • This pages remembers your locations when you come back (using local storage, your locations are never sent to the server).
This is updated daily with the Johns Hopkins CSSE data, which is typically released around midnight Central.
