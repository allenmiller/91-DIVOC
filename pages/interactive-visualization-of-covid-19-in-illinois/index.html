Interactive Visualization of COVID-19 in Illinois

Data-forward visualization of COVID-19 data in Illinois. Updated daily from the Illinois Department of Public Health (IDPH).
The visualizations on this page contains data from the Illinois Department of Health visualized in an graphical, interactive way to help make sense of the pandemic in Illinois.
  • These graphs are not just images. Hover over any data point to see the data behind it.
  • This first graph shows the raw data, while the second graph normalizes the countries by population. Both graphs uses the same data from the Illinois Dept. of Health.
  • There are over 1,000,000,000 different graphs to explore. View the guide to this visualization to get started.
  • This visualization is updated daily from The Illinois Department of Public Health's official website.
